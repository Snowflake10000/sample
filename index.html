Most expensive cat breeds in the world


Introduction

In the world of feline companionship, cats are often cherished for their unique personalities. Some cat breeds come with a hefty price tag, reflecting their rarity, distinctive features, and prestigious lineage.

Here are the 3 most expensive cat breeds:

  1. Ashera

    Price: $22,000-$125,000
    They are large, friendly, and with a dog-like personality. The feline is part African leopard, part African serval, and part domestic tabby. It is also known for its fondness for heated blankets.

  2. Savannah

    Price: $10,000-$25,000
    A cross between the tall, lean, and big-eared wild serval and a domestic cat, its price range in part results from how many generations it has been crossed with a domestic cat.
